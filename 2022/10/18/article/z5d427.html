Weekly Review 2022-10-10 to 2022-10-16

Tuesday, October 18, 2022 by Loura | #article | #life#weekly
"Wow, did the week fly by. That was probably helped along by the fact that I had a 48-hour migraine which left Wednesday and Thursday in a blur.

House

Monday was a holiday that I had off of work. That let me get to some long overdue painting that is needed in sun room since we redid the windows. I sanded, used wood filler, and applied a layer of stain blocking to the wood. Hopefully that will stop any pine knots from bleeding through. Lastly a layer of white primer went up. It'll still take us weeks to get everything finished. My tentative goal is before Thanksgiving. I continued painting in the sunroom on Sunday and have the first coat of off-white on the walls!

Homeschooling

My record journal is looking fantastic this year. I try to fill it in weekly with pictures and a record of what we've done. This year I bought stickers and an instant printer to make the pages more colorful and lively.

Work

One thing about having a migraine on a work day, is that my mind is in no position to do coding. I've tried it and it just doesn't work out. What I can do however, is plan. So I cleaned out project management system, re-organized tasks, made some documentation and got together a better plan for the next few weeks. Bonus points, it made me excited to get back to coding at the end of the week.
