Weekly Review 2022-09-26 to 2022-10-02

Monday, October 3, 2022 by Loura | #article | #life #weekly

What a week! Getting back into the groove of things after a traveling is rough. Especially if getting back into the groove means getting the kids back into our homeschooling routine and getting myself back and ready for the busy season at work. Yikes! But the trip out to Western NY was wonderful and it was great to see family that we don't normally get a chance to see.

Homeschooling Update

On the homeschooling side of things, I'm still struggling to fit in all the wonderful things I so optimistically planned for in the summer. It never fails, at least in my last three years of doing this, that by the time fall comes around I figure out that 1. some of my choices just won't work and I need to pivot and 2. The kids always take longer than I plan to finish things up.

So right now, I've had to switch around math for both kids and I'm trying to figure how to get my social studies choice to fit in the schedule. That might be one I just let go for now or simply watch the videos and discuss.

Work Update

Ah, legacy data. Thou art a pain. Since the application rewrite is well underway and since the main driver of this rewrite was an underlying database architecture issue ... mapping the old data to its new structure is taking longer than I initially anticipated. Mainly because I wasn't satisfied in just dumping the data somewhere and calling it good. So that is going to take up some time next week and I've officially missed my personal deadline of wrapping up this feature by October 1st. Le sigh.

Life Update

Why is finding time to take care of yourself so hard? I know I need to work out, I want to work out, but ... I know I need to eat nutritious things and I want too, but ... It's hard, I'm busy, and I parenting pre-teens. So right now, I'll celebrate my small wins and find joy in that. I fit in a workout both Monday and Wednesday and taught Tai Chi on Sunday. I also made some healthy vegetarian meals. I did manage to clean some parts of my house and make the budget for this month. For this next week I really want to focus on getting back into stretching daily and taking 15 min of quiet time for myself.
