It was my freshmen year at University in 2003 and I remember my first Computer Science class. I was super intimidated and the room being filled with almost all men didn't help. It remained that way throughout my years of school. I often wondered, when I entered into a new class, where the other women were. Computer Science has such a rich history with women that I only learned about years after I left school. Why aren't these stories acknowledged more? Why aren't they shared in middle school and in high school? Or even in college level classes? 

Pictures and stories like these would have helped when I was younger.
